Gesture Theory is an industry-leading, full-service design and development firm headquartered in New York, NY.

We have extensive experience building IOS applications, consumer websites, and other interactive tools and interfaces. As a team of multi-disciplinary experts. GEsture Theory emphasizes smart, elegant and lightweight user interactions through fundamental principles of User Experience (UX). Visual design and Development Strategy. Our philosophy is loosely based around an Agile software development process, which enables our different teams to work efficiently and in parallel, while also ensuring our products meet and exceed the needs of our cleints.
